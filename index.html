About Park Sky:

Created to allows users to search for national parks for a given visitation date in the US. It not only displays general information and a brief summary for each park, but it also presents the user with a star chart and for what the night sky will look like at that park on a specific date, any near earth objects that will be passing the Earth on the specified date, and the weather forecast for that day.
